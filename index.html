Our Chapter

The SIG Center hosts a large and active ACM SIGGRAPH Student Chapter.

  • Penn's SIGGRAPH Chapter is a computer graphics oriented group which focuses on fostering a community of students interested in animation, computer science, games, general graphics, and VFX.
  • This chapter was recognized with the 2018-2019 Student Chapter Excellence Award:
  • Some of SIGGRAPH's activities include a Demo Reel Night, weekly talks on computer graphics topics, tutorials, panels, and movie nights.
